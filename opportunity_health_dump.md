`★ Insight ─────────────────────────────────────`
- **Why title-based dedupe is unreliable here**: rows 88 and 89 differ by a single stress accent (`петанку` vs `пета́нку`) — a literal `GROUP BY title` would treat them as distinct, and `lower(title)` won't help either. Real dedupe needs Unicode normalization (NFKD + strip combining marks) or fuzzy matching on a normalized form. Worth knowing if you write a cleanup script.
- **Why mistags happened the way they did**: the seed's classifier is almost certainly using the title + a snippet, not the full article. "Безкоштовно" + "ветерани" → strong signal for `rehabilitation`, even when the actual offering is sushi or shoes. A second-pass classifier with the body text (or just a "is this a discount/promo unrelated to health?" rejection step) would catch most of these.
- **What the visible bias toward `rehabilitation` tells you about the source corpus**: most of these come from `dduvs.edu.ua`, `nashemisto.dp.ua`, `suspilne.media`, and Dnipro-area Facebook pages — outlets that report on sport-as-rehab and military-veteran programs. The data shape mirrors the source shape; if `recovery` (substance/behavioural) is genuinely a category you want filled, you'll need a different source list (addiction-recovery NGOs, AA/NA Ukraine, etc.).
